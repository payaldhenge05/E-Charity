Our Medical Stock

Paracetamol

Paracetamol is a commonly used medicine that can help treat pain and reduce a high temperature (fever). It's typically used to relieve mild or moderate pain, such as headaches, toothache or sprains, and reduce fevers caused by illnesses such as colds and flu.

Aspirin

Aspirin is used to reduce fever and relieve mild to moderate pain from conditions such as muscle aches, toothaches, common cold, and headaches. It may also be used to reduce pain and swelling in conditions such as arthritis.

Febrex Plus

Febrex Plus Tablet is a Tablet manufactured by Indoco Remedies Ltd. It is commonly used for the diagnosis or treatment of Running nose, itching, watery eyes, common cold, flu.It provides relief from symptoms such as headache, sore throat, runny nose.

Rinifol

Rinifol Capsule contains vitamins (such as Vitamin B5, B6, B7, B9, and B12), minerals , and probiotics.These capsules are basically a preparation that not only normalizes gut organisms/flora but also provides essential vitamins and minerals to the body.

Cetirizine

Cetirizine is known as a non-drowsy antihistamine. It's much less likely to make you feel sleepy than some other antihistamines.Cetirizine is an antihistamine medicine that helps the symptoms of allergies. It's used to treat: hay fever. conjunctivitis (red, itchy eye).

Zeal

Zeal Tablet belongs to a group of medicines called proton pump inhibitors.It is commonly used for the diagnosis or treatment of duodenal ulcers, stomach acid , gastroesophageal reflux disease. It has some side effects such as Asthenia,Cough,Abdominal pain.

Nimek-Para

It is prescribed mainly to treat pain and relieve discomfort caused by conditions like tooth pain, arthritis, period pain and other types of short-term pains. It helps treat pain by blocking the chemical messenger in the brain responsible for causing fever, pain and inflammation.

Vomikind MD 4

Vomikind MD 4 Tablet 10's belongs to a group of medications called anti-emetics used to prevent nausea and vomiting associated with conditions like cancer chemotherapy, radiotherapy, surgery, pregnancy and motion sickness in adults and children above 4 years of age.
